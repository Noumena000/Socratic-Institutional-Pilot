# Questions for the Institutional AI Lead

- What exactly is the supported development surface: API, agent builder, workflow platform, or internal skill framework?
- Can the service return schema-constrained JSON?
- Is there a sandbox using fictional data?
- What learner data may be submitted, logged, retained, and deleted?
- Where must an approved application be hosted?
- What authentication and authorization services are available?
- Who reviews privacy, security, accessibility, and LMS integration?
- What is the smallest appropriate first pilot?
- What written agreement will define background intellectual property and permitted institutional use?
